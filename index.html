Sebina's Plan

Sebina’s plan to overcome the imminent menace of the Goddess of the destruction consists of a specialized training program with the goal to help the inhabitants of Aden to train themselves in various kinds of combat situations hence the reason why it’s necessary to practice in all kinds of environments across the world.

In the past people were able to master the usage of their classes and skills in one specific area or two, but since not even Sebina herself knows the limits of the power of the goddess Shilen she requests you train yourself all over Aden.

Upon talking with Sebina in any major town you will get teleported to a random location with a flagged status and ready to challenge the players that will start to randomly appear around you. Dont let your guard down or else you will perish miserably!!!

Relive the Glory Days

One of our goals while making frontier was to fix the eternal issue of lack of participation in the olympiad matches, and because of this we’ve decided to make the olys much more friendlier for everyone by increasing your starting weekly olympiad points up to five times more, with this measures you wont have any issue in practice your best setups and eventually start to overcome your foes at the olympiads.

Be 1vs1 against your class or 1vs1 against a different class than yours or the brand new 3vs3 with any class setup the olympiads in frontier are going to be the most approachable ones yet! and who knows? you might win some awesome rewards too!! (hint: check the rewards in-game)

Back to the Basics!!

“Urawaza” is a Japanese word which mean simply “Tricks” like the old school “infinite lives” tricks we’ve used to cherish a lot playing old retro video games, Lineage II is a massive game with lots and lots of content also has it’s share of tricks as well, and despite Frontier is a PvP based server there’s always room for surprises and all kinds of tactics.

Everything in the game is enabled this includes, quests, dungeons, raid bosses, etc. This means you can actually get some edge against your opponents and enemies if you know a lot about Lineage II!

Server Features

  • Chronicle High Five Part 5 "Signs Of Destruction"
  • Newly created characters start with Lv 85.
  • Maximum of 3 subclasses are allowed with a maximum level is 80.
  • Rates x5000.
  • GM Shop “Squishy Rodent” includes all armors, weapons, epic jewels, etc.
  • Services NPC “Rascal” offers all kinds of services such as noble, pvp gear, warehouse, etc.
  • Dynamic PvP Area System which allows for constant pvp free-for-all battles across the world of Aden.
  • Retail-like classes and balances. High Five 10% rule working at it’s fullest for all classes!.
  • MP potions disabled, however your natural MP regeneration rate has been increased x3 times.
  • Game Store “Frontier Vault” which offers Cosmetic and Enhancement items in exchange of FP(Frontier Points).
  • Maximum Enchant +16/+20*.
  • Attribute System up to Lv 7.
  • Sieges and Territory Wars every 2 weeks.
  • Olympiads starting points increased x5 times.
  • 3vs3 Olympiads enabled.
  • No enchant limits on the Olympiads.
  • Total Anarchy, no PK penalties.
  • Raid Bosses, Quests, Dungeons, Monsters and Instances working.
  • Buffer system .yanbuffer with enchanted buffs.
  • No farming!, only it’s necessary to farm Clan Reputation Points (CRP) by doing Fortresses and Castle Sieges!.
